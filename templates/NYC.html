Introduction

How many properties are sold in NYC every year? Which types of properties are mostly sold? To answer these questions, this project is going to analyze and visualize the data of the sold properties in New York City during a certain time period.




Findings

First, in general, most sold properties were for living purposes. Second, by looking at the above charts, unlike some industries that have clear seasonal variations (i.e. farming, Halloween retailers), I found that the real estate industry doesn’t have such a pattern. If you see the total sales property sales amount and the number of sales charts , no month always stayed on a peak or a valley. That means the trading volume wasn’t affected by seasons or months. It was constantly changing.
